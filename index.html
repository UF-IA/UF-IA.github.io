University of Florida

Who Are We

Innovation Ambassadors is a new ambassador program with the purposes of serving as student representatives of the UF Engineering Innovation Institute and encouraging a multidisciplinary innovative culture among students. We plan to achieve this by engaging students across campus in three ways, executed by our three committees: Resource Promotion, Community Outreach, and Event Planning.

Event Planning

This committee focuses on planning IAâ€™s own events to foster innovation. The possibilities for events are endless, but include: guest speaker presentations/panels, skill-based workshops, student presentations/panels, networking events, design-a-thons, and more.

Resource Promotion

This committee focuses on spreading the many existing innovation-based resources at UF which are often poorly represented to students. To do this, this committee will develop and manage an IA website (website developers needed), manage IA social media accounts, stay updated on all innovation resources, and any other methods seen fit.

Student Outreach

This committee focuses on developing a community of innovators at UF, and facilitating relationships between students and ambassadors. This committee will manage communication between students and ambassadors. This committee will also build external awareness about IA through tabling, club fairs, and other student engagement.
