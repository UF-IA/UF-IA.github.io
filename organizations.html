SILICON VALLEY PROGRAM

  • The Silicon Valley Program is an immersion program offered during the spring semester providing students with an intense exposure to entrepreneurship and the entrepreneurial eco-system in Silicon Valley (including San Francisco)
BIG IDEA GATOR BUSINESS PLAN COMPETITION

  • The Big Idea Gator Business Plan Competition was created to help students grow their business ideas.
  • A four month competition in the spring where teams of students begin by creating growth-oriented business models
GATOR HATCHERY

  • The Gator Hatchery is a Student Incubator that offers student entrepreneurs workspace, office support, mentors and other resources necessary for a startup to succeed faster.
  • Hatchery businesses will have access to the Entrepreneurship Center’s network of individuals, companies and other resources fledgling companies may find valuable.
ENTREPRENEURSHIP COLLECTIVE

  • Have a wide array of roles consisting of operations, management, marketing, finance, strategy, planning, social media, technology, photography, videography, blogging and more
