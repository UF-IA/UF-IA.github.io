EGN2020C (Engineering Design and Society)

  • Create a prototype to solve a problem

  • Team project
EGN4643/6642

  • Explore the vital role engineers play in the problem solving and innovation process
  • Looks at starting entrepreneurial ventures, completing research, executing projects, and working for multinational companies
  • Examine the innovator’s mindset
EGS4100/EGS6100

  • Applies divergent thinking, which is creating numerous solutions to a problem, instead of following a strict set of rules to arrive at one correct solution (convergent thinking)
  • Most engineering courses place an emphasis on convergent thinking
  • Students will cultivate multiple forms of intelligence, become more aware of their thinking process
EGN 4641/EGN 6640

  • This course introduces engineering students to the concepts and practices of entrepreneurial thinking and entrepreneurship. Using lectures, case studies, business plans, and student presentations, the course teaches life skills in entrepreneurial thought and action that students apply in starting companies or driving innovative, cutting edge projects in large companies.
